Hacker Summary

Hackers are people who use technology, computers, and the Internet as a means to infiltrate computers, networks, and websites illegally. Hackers are interested in electronic operations, and they seek to obtain confidential, financial and commercial information in any way possible, whether by hacking or fraud. Hackers can be classified into several categories, some of them work illegally and aim to destroy computers, networks and websites, and some of them work legally and work to test and improve the security of the system. There is also a category called "hackers" who aim to penetrate computer systems and networks to obtain confidential information and destroy data. In addition to their illegal activities, hackers can be highly technically skilled people who use these skills for their own benefit and the benefit of society. Hackers can improve cyber security and develop free and open source software. Despite this, the illegal activities of hackers pose a threat to electronic security and privacy, so digital security systems are strengthened to protect information from breaches and fraud, and hackers who commit digital crimes are held accountable and brought to justice.
